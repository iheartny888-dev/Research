Evaluating the Impact of Phonological Changes on Perceived Linguistic Naturalness

Study Information

This is a research survey conducted to evaluate how patterns in word formation contribute to the perceived naturalness of a given text. You will be presented with pairs of text samples and will be asked to judge which seems more natural to you.

Your responses will be:

  • Recorded and stored for later analysis
  • Used only for the purposes of the study
  • Associated only with your provided name

The survey will take approximately 10-15 minutes to complete.
